About Blessing Junior School

Our Story

Blessing Junior School was established with a vision of being a centre of love, care, academic excellence and buil a God fearing generation

Our mission is to "train up a child" in the way they should go, providing not only academic knowledge but also moral guidance and life skills that will serve them throughout their lives.

Our Values

  • Integrity and Honesty
  • Passion and Quality
  • Learning and Team work
